# Target Company Identification 

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on identifying and validating 25 high-quality manufacturing companies suitable for engineering (B.E / B.Tech) students. The goal is to ensure that each company has real operational presence and aligns with specific technical industry segments.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* To research and shortlist companies with strong engineering relevance
* To filter out companies that do not meet strict manufacturing and operational criteria
* To build a structured dataset of target companies for career and industry analysis

---

## 📍 Selected City

Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

---

## 🏭 Selected Industry Segments

* Medical Devices
* Industrial Sensors & Instrumentation

---

## 🔍 Methodology

1. Conducted initial research on 75–100 companies using:

   * LinkedIn
   * IndiaMART
   * Company websites
2. Verified each company’s:

   * Manufacturing or R&D presence in Chennai
   * Industry relevance
3. Applied strict filtering criteria to shortlist 25 companies

---

## ✅ Filtering Criteria

Companies were included only if they:

* Have manufacturing or R&D operations in Chennai
* Belong to the selected industry segments
* Offer roles relevant to B.E / B.Tech students
* Are active and operational

Companies were excluded if they:

* Are only distributors or sales offices
* Belong to unrelated industries
* Lack verified operational presence

## 📈 Key Insights

* A large number of companies were found to be distributors rather than manufacturers
* Mid-sized companies offered better alignment with engineering roles
* Verification of operational presence was critical in filtering
