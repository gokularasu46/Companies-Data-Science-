## REGULATORY LANDSCAPE FOR BENGALURU BIOTECH

### Key Regulatory Bodies
1. **Department of Biotechnology (DBT):** Funding, incentives, policy
2. **Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO):** Drug/device approvals
3. **SEBI/BSE-NSE:** For listed companies
4. **State Biotech Association:** Industry advocacy

### Compliance Requirements
- **GMP/GCP Certifications:** Mandatory for manufacturing
- **FDA/EMA Approvals:** Required for export (USA/Europe)
- **ISO 13485:** For medical device companies
- **AYUSH Compliance:** For traditional medicine-related diagnostics
- **Data Privacy (DPIIT):** For biodata-related companies

### Government Incentives
- **DBT Schemes:** R&D funding, training programs
- **SEZ Benefits:** 15% corporate tax for 10 years
- **State Incentives:** Land, infrastructure support
- **Patent Support:** IP filing assistance
